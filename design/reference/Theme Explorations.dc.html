Theme lineup — five personalities on one token structure
The system: one skeleton, five outfits
Every theme fills the same token roles — background, surface tint, accent, secondary accent, text, success/warning — over the untouched Liquid Glass substrate. Layout, navigation, and behavior never change. Each ships light + dark.

Recommended lead: Pop! — it answers the "too businessy and sad" complaint most directly. Dialed to sherbet, not candy aisle: cream grounds, soft ink text, saturated color reserved for accents and moments. Cozy Home is the runner-up if Pop! reads too loud in the key screens.
type: SF Pro Rounded everywhere (system, zero shipping cost) · motion: springy, tactile · mascot presence varies per theme
Fen — proposed direction
A geometric marsh fox: flat shapes, big ears, no outlines. Recolors per theme, clean enough for adults. Presence dial per theme — Arcade leans in (game sprite), Fresh & Dusk stay mascot-light.
